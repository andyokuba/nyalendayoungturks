Who We Are


Mobile for reproductive health(m4rh) is a program that was applied to the Rwandan context by the Ministry of Health in partnership Imbuto Foundation, UNFPA and other partners, members of the Adolescent Sexual Reproductive Health & Family planning technical working groups. It was designed to give young people in Rwanda access to accurate information about sexual and reproductive health using digital technology.
